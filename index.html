看 AI 如何解读会议 See how AI reads between the lines

一段看似达成共识的对话，背后隐藏着完全不同的真实态度。 A conversation that seems like consensus — but hides three very different stances.

张三Alice："我觉得可以试试""I think we could give it a try"


李四Bob："原则上同意，但需要更多数据""I agree in principle, but we need more data"


王五Carol：（沉默 10 秒）(10 sec silence)

王五Carol："好吧...""Fine..."

态度分析Stance

张三 — 试探性支持Alice — Tentative support

"试试" 表明不确定性，非坚定支持"Give it a try" signals uncertainty, not commitment

潜台词挖掘Subtext

李四 — 有条件同意Bob — Conditional agreement

"原则上" 是经典限定词，暗示存在保留意见"In principle" is a classic hedge — signals hidden reservations

价值密度 9/10Value 9/10

王五 — 被动接受Carol — Passive acceptance

长时间沉默 + "好吧" = 不情愿的妥协Long silence + "Fine…" = reluctant compliance

01
关键词语敏感度Keyword Sensitivity
限定词、程度副词、转折信号——"但是"后面才是真实立场Hedges, qualifiers, pivots — what follows "but" is the real stance
02
语气情感识别Tone & Sentiment
判断确定性程度和情感倾向：支持、反对、保留还是质疑Certainty levels and emotional direction: support, oppose, reserve, question
03
潜台词挖掘Subtext Mining
表面赞同实际反对、未说出的顾虑、刻意回避的敏感点Surface agreement masking dissent, unspoken concerns, avoided topics
04
矛盾异常捕捉Contradiction Detection
前后观点不一致、突然的话题转换、不合理的沉默Inconsistent positions, abrupt topic shifts, unusual silence
05
价值密度评分Value Density Scoring
1-10 分量化每段发言的信息价值，聚焦真正重要的内容1-10 scoring per utterance — focus on what actually matters
06
情感态度图谱Sentiment Map
